**Tagline (60 chars):** AI meal plans with recipes and a grocery list in 30 seconds

**Description:**
What's For Dinner generates personalized weekly meal plans with full recipes and a consolidated grocery list. Tell it your dietary preferences, allergies, household size, and budget — get a complete week of meals in 30 seconds.

Free 3-day plan, no credit card. Subscribers get a fresh 7-day plan delivered to their inbox every Sunday.

**Text (paste in the "text" field for Show HN posts):**
I built this because my family had the same "what's for dinner?" conversation every night. Recipe apps have thousands of options — that's the problem, not the solution. You still have to pick something, check the pantry, figure out portions, and build a grocery list yourself.

What's For Dinner is different: you tell it your dietary preferences, household size, allergies, and budget, and it generates a complete week of meals — with full recipes and one consolidated grocery list — in about 30 seconds.
